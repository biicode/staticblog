]]> We are happy to announce that we have come out on top of the 2013 IBM SmartCamp in Spain, a competition of startups around the globe, where the winners are rewarded with mentoring, services, access to industry experts and partnership opportunities.

Some of the best startups from around the country submitted their projects to the program, but in the end it all came down to five finalists who presented their pitch in an exclusive event at the offices of Madrid International Lab on October 24th. Manuel Arrufat and Diego Rodríguez-Losada, who represented the entry for biicode, got the opportunity to wow the audience with our project, among them esteemed judges and professionals of the industry.

Finally, after two days of hard work and excitement, we could proudly present the winner: biicode.

As soon as we received the news our whole team rushed to the event with a big smile on our faces in order to celebrate the achievement and chat with last year’s winner. We not only won the official IBM SmartCamp Award, but also the People’s favorite award which we appreciate especially, as it shows that our fans and supporters care about us the way we care about them.

Diego Rodríguez-Losada, biicode’s CTO, said:

“Being selected as a finalist is an amazing opportunity and a great honor. It gives us the chance to showcase some of the great work that’s coming out of biicode in terms of technology and innovation.”

This entire experience is an epic opportunity for us and we are proud to compete in the European Finals in Vienna on November 8th. There was some great competition and an amazing experience to stand in the spotlight with the other startups.

We would like to extend a special thank you to all of you who have supported us, we would never be here had you not given us the chance to prove ourselves.
