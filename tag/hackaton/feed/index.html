]]> Last friday, we had the chance to host and participate in a CPython workshop at our offices in Madrid. James Powell, one of the greatest Pythonists of our times, came straight from his New York based Python group to share his knowledge and blow our minds.

In a real-time projection he guided us through the unknown world of CPython, showing us the C source code invoked by calling Python. He gave us insights on how to read the source and on the process of contributing to CPython, among other topics.

While commenting and taking notes we could also discover interesting details of Python’s implementation and its internals to better understand the technology applied.

The developers and our team had a good time learning and coding. And whenever we felt we had seen enough code, playing Ping Pong was the right thing to do to have fun and continue with a fresh mind.

Apart from the Hackathon we had the pleasure to get to know James outside our office while grabbing something to eat and talking about his background and vision: What a great guy!

We want to thank all participants for coming by and making the Hackathon a great day. Feel free to join any future Hackathons or events. :)
