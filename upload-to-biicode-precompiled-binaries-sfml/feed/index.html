This works, but dependencies.bii is not aware of your compilation settings nor your system/architecture, so its hard to configure the setup to download the required binaries only (Windows x64 release, for example). Note how my example downloads all the binaries and then decides to what library it has to link to.

We are working in a hook system to configure all those things in a simple way, I hope we will be able to upload big precompiled libraries to biicode in the near future.
